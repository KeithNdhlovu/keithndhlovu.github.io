Mission

Be the change we want to see in the world - Mahatma Ghandi.

Education

We, as Acornhoek movers believe that, the average child has the pottential to be great and excell at what they do, as long as they get the naccesary guidance to help them achieve that. We have tutorship programs starting from grade 8-12 teaching learners in subjects they find diffuclt. We have programs which enhance the talents of young people. We also do charitable work, such as fundraising for learners who are from poor backgrounds to have proper school uniforms

We are one

One man cant change the world, but can certainly be the change they wish to see in the world. Imagine if that is so, every member of this organisation has the same mentality and different backgrounds, but we all have a common goal, which is to uplift our community.

Passionate

We know that there are still people who believe that for someone to help them achieve something great, there has to be some strings attached, Well all we want to do is show people that there is good in the world, and that we do this out of the goodnes of our hearts and that theres no greater reward than for someone to approach you and say "Thanks for everything, had it not been for you, i would'nt be where i am today"
